Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kharcha Diary handles user information. By using our Service, you agree to this policy.

Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.

Kharcha Diary does not require you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
All financial records including expenses, income, and investments that you create are stored locally on your device only.
Data is retained locally on your device for up to 1 year, unless you delete it earlier.
We do not have access to your data at any time.
We do not transmit, upload, or sync your data to any external servers.


Data Storage
All data created within Kharcha Diary (including expense records, income entries, investment logs, and tags) is stored using your device's local storage.

Your data remains entirely under your control.
Data is stored locally on your device for a maximum of 1 year.
After this period, data may be automatically removed by the app.
Deleting the app may permanently delete your stored data unless you have created a backup.
We do not provide cloud storage or synchronization services.


Data Export
Kharcha Diary allows you to export your financial records to an Excel (.xlsx) file.

Exported files are saved directly to your device's local storage (Downloads folder).
Exported files are never transmitted to any server or third party.
You are fully responsible for any exported files once they are saved to your device.


Backup and Restore
Kharcha Diary provides a local backup and restore feature.

You can create a backup of your data as a .db file and save it to your device's local storage.
You can restore your data by selecting a previously created backup file from your device.
Backup files are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.
You are fully responsible for managing and securing your backup files.


Storage Access
Kharcha Diary requests access to device storage for the following purposes only:

To save exported Excel (.xlsx) files to your device's Downloads folder.
To save database backup (.db) files to your device's local storage.
To read backup files from your device storage for the restore feature.
We do not access, read, or modify any other files on your device outside of these specific operations.
